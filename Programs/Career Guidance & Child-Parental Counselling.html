Career Guidance & Child/Parental Counselling

Career Exploration Guidance

We provide comprehensive guidance and resources to help students explore various career paths. From aptitude assessments to informational interviews with professionals, we support students in making informed decisions about their future.Our counseling services address emotional and developmental concerns that may impact students' academic performance and overall well-being. Our trained counselors provide a safe and supportive environment for students to express themselves and develop coping strategies.

What do We guide ?

Career Exploration

Canaan Academy offers career counseling to help students explore options, based on their interests, strengths, and aspirations, guiding informed decisions.

Skill Development

We teach essential skills such as resume writing, interview preparation, and networking, empowering students to navigate the professional world with confidence and competence.

Emotional Support

Our counseling services offer emotional support, addressing any challenges they may face and helping them develop resilience and coping strategies

Goal Setting

We assist students in setting realistic goals and creating action plans to achieve them, fostering a sense of purpose and direction in their academic and personal lives.

Why Canaan group is best fit for your child ?

Curriculum and Teaching Approach

The Canaan Play school have a well-designed curriculum that focuses on holistic development, incorporating play-based learning, hands-on activities, and age-appropriate educational methods.

Qualified and Experienced Teachers & Facilitators

The Canaan Group employs teachers, Facilitators and counsellors who are highly qualified, experienced, and trained in early childhood education and all other training and skill development fields.

Parental Involvement and Communication

Canaan Group encourages active parental involvement and maintains open communication. This can help you stay informed about your child's progress and participate in their learning journey.

Targeted Curriculum

Canaan's teen-focused soft skills programs are likely tailored to address the unique developmental needs and challenges faced by adolescents.The curriculum cover essential life skills, such as communication, problem-solving, emotional intelligence, and decision-making, which are crucial for the holistic growth of teenagers.

Experienced Facilitators

We employ facilitators who are specifically trained and experienced in working with teenage students.These facilitators can understand the perspectives, concerns, and learning styles of teens, and they can create an engaging and relevant learning environment.

Interactive Approach

Canaan's soft skills programs for teens utilize interactive teaching methods, such as role-playing, group discussions, and experiential learning activities.This can help teenagers actively participate, apply the concepts, and develop practical skills that they can readily integrate into their daily lives.

Peer Learning and Networking

Canaan's programs may provide opportunities for teenagers to collaborate, share experiences, and learn from their peers.This peer-to-peer interaction can foster a sense of community, mutual understanding, and support among the participants.

Holistic Development

Canaan group programs designed to complement and enhance the academic curriculum, addressing the overall development of teenagers beyond just academic performance.This holistic approach can prepare teenagers for the demands of higher education, the workforce, and adulthood.
